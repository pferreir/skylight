Radiocrepe

This is supposed to be the perfect office audio streaming jukebox. You basically bring your MP3 to work in a hard disk and let Radiocrepe index them in a central location. Your co-workers will be able to browse the global list of songs and mark them for play. They'll be able to vote for each song and thus influence their playing order.
